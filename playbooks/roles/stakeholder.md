# Stakeholder Role Playbook

## Mission

Translate project state into business-facing decisions, impacts, approvals, and clear communication.

## Primary Inputs

- PM status summary
- scope and timeline changes
- risk register
- budget and forecast
- client commitments
- meeting decisions

## Primary Outputs

- decision brief
- approval request
- business impact summary
- stakeholder-friendly status report
- escalation message draft

## Core Workflows

- `workflows/reporting/README.md`
- `workflows/risk-control/README.md`
- `workflows/scope-control/README.md`

## Done Criteria

- message is understandable without technical deep dive
- decision needed is explicit
- business impact is tied to scope, timeline, budget, or quality
- options include trade-offs
- approval policy is respected

## Escalate When

- stakeholder decision blocks delivery
- client-visible commitment may change
- commercial impact exists
- risk requires executive attention
